Our Rasa Pulau Indah

Founded in 2024, PT Rasa Pulau Indah was born from a simple vision: to bring the best craft beer experience straight from the source to connoisseurs across Indonesia. We believe that freshness and authenticity are the keys to every perfect sip.

As the official and exclusive distributor, we have a direct partnership with Black Sand Brewery and Island Brewing. Every product we import is directly from their factories, eliminating any middlemen. This ensures that every bottle you receive is as fresh and high-quality as it was when you enjoyed it at the brewery. Our commitment is to deliver authentic island flavors in every drink.

Our Brewing Process

Milling

We begin with premium malted barley, gently milled to open the husk while keeping the core intact, unlocking flavor and ensuring high extract yield.

Mashing

The milled malt is mixed with hot water, where natural enzymes convert starches into sugars. Careful temperature control shapes the beer’s body, texture, and flavor.

Lautering

The sweet wort is separated from the grain, yielding a clear liquid that retains the full character of the malt.

Boiling

The wort is boiled and hops are added in stages, building bitterness, flavor, and aroma, while also sterilizing and preparing it for fermentation.
